Who Are we?

Meet TOWAN, a stoner rock band that is not your average group. With a strong disdain for the traditional education system/societal system, they channel their energy into their music, creating a sound that is both raw and liberating. Their rehearsals & shows serve not only as a way to perfect their craft, but also as an escape from the stresses of daily life. This band of five members come together to create a truly unique, fun and entertaining experience..
